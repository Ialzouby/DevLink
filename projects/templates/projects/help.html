Help & Support

Find answers to common questions about using DevLink.

To edit your profile, follow these steps:

  1. Click on your profile picture at the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown menu.
  3. Update your information and save changes.

To create a project:

  1. Go to the homepage and click "Create Project".
  2. Fill in project details such as title, description, and skills required.
  3. Click "Submit" to create your project.

You can send messages by:

  1. Going to "Messaging" from the navbar.
  2. Selecting a user or starting a new conversation.
  3. Typing your message and clicking "Send".

To manage notifications:

  1. Click the bell icon on the navbar.
  2. View unread notifications.
  3. Click on a notification to mark it as read.

To delete a project:

  1. Go to the project page.
  2. Click the "Delete" button.
  3. Confirm your choice to remove the project permanently.
